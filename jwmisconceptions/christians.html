Are Jehovah's Witnesses Christians?

Yes, Jehovah Witnesses are Christians. How?

  • We try to imitate Jesus teachings and his way of life very closely. He left a model for us to follow —1Peter 2:21.
  • We believe that Jesus is the only way to salvation, “there is not another name under heaven that has been given among men by which we must get saved.” —Acts 4:12.
  • When ones become Jehovah's Witnesses, they are baptized in the name of Jesus. We offer prayers in Jesus name —Matthew 28:18, 19 ,John 15:16
  • We are different from many religious organizations that are called Christian. For example, we believe that Jesus is God's son, not part of a Trinity. We do not believe that there is any evidence in the Bible that supports the teaching of wicked people being tormented in hell or religious leaders having a higher title or ranking in their religious activities.
